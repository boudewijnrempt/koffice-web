Chapter 1. Overview

1.1. Introduction to KPlato

The purpose of the K PLAnning TOol (KPlato) project is to develop a Project Management tool for Linux that will allow people to effectively plan and schedule projects in many different fields, including software development, manufacturing processes, and construction.

KPlato is being developed for the K Desktop Environment.

Detailed information on the development of KPlato can be obtained from the following resources:

  • The KPlato website: www.koffice.org/kplato

  • The KPlato mailing list: <kplato@kde.org>

  • The mailing list archives: master.kde.org/pipermail/kplato
