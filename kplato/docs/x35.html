1.2. Document Scope

This purpose of this document is to combine the discussions on the KPlato mailing list and provide a single source of information on the discussions and decisions made to date.

The items in this document are presented in a relatively informal manner. Although many of the items discussed here are expected to guide the design of KPlato this should not be considered a formal requirements or design document.

The intent of this document is to reflect the current discussions and decisions made on the KPlato mailing list. If there are any omissions or errors please report them to the author, or on the KPlato mailing list, <kplato@kde.org>
